Apple ‘HomePod’ speaker to take on Amazon, Google

By Mohamed Sobhy -06/09/2017

Apple will later this year release a ‘HomePod’ music-centric smart home speaker, challenging a market currently dominated by Amazon and Google in its latest move to weave deeper into people’s lives.HomePod, aided by Apple’s Siri digital assistant, will be priced at $349 when it begins shipping in December in the United States, Australia and Britain, the tech giant announced at its annual Worldwide Developers Conference.“We really believe it is going to take your home music experience to the next level,” Apple chief executive Tim Cook said as he unveiled it.
